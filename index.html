About Me

I am Raffael Putra Hermawan, known as Ell —a 15-year-old student pursuing dual expertise in Cybersecurity and Python Development simultaneously. I am currently in Grade 10, Semester 2, committed to self-driven growth in technology.

My journey began in Grade 7 when I first discovered programming through Termux on Android —experimenting with scripts before I fully understood what I was doing. That curiosity evolved in Grade 8, when the video game Watch Dogs 2 introduced me to the world of cybersecurity and inspired me to explore how real systems are analyzed and tested.

Since then, I have committed to continuous self-learning through online courses, competitions, certifications, and hands-on experimentation. My studies span programming, advanced networking, Linux administration, wireless security, and offensive security research.

On March 9, 2026, I formally began applying my skills in legal and responsible cybersecurity practice —committed fully to ethical principles and responsible disclosure.
